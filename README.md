# MTG-Analysis
An end-to-end exploration of Magic the Gathering cards

## Step 1 - Data Collection
There are multiple databases of Magic the Gathering cards available online including the official [Magic the Gathering Gatherer](https://gatherer.wizards.com), [Scryfall](https://scryfall.com/docs/api/bulk-data), [MTGJSON](https://mtgjson.com) eBay-owned [TCGPlayer](https://www.tcgplayer.com), and many others.
While many of these databases exist, Scryfall and MTGJSON are the two most accessible databases with easy bulk data exportation. For my analysis, I chose to source my card data from Scryfall because on top of its bulk data exportation tools, it is toted to be ["the best and most complete Magic card database"](https://draftsim.com/best-mtg-deck-builder/) and it is integrated into [Moxfield](https://moxfield.com), one of the most popular deck-building websites.
